Keyword: Assessment

This graph shows the actors involved in articles mentioning 'assessment' in their titles. Considering that under the current laws regarding education in the United States, the federal government is responsible for reviewing the assessments that are given at the state level it is not suprising to see that United States and the Department of Education are some of the most mentioned actors. To get a better idea of why these actors were involved with assessments in the United States, and how the articles depicted assessments, I chose to look at the average tone of the articles grouped by actors.

Here each bar represents the average tone for all of the articles involving a particular actor from the top 15 actors above. I chose to exclude the actors 'College', 'University', and 'Professor' as I was more interested in the assessments being given at the K-12 level in the United States educational system. The actors who had the most mentions from this sample of actors like School and United States have nearly neutral tones, suggesting that there is likely a great share of criticism and praise of assessments in the United States media. To further describe the media being presented on the topic of assessments in the US, I decided to look at the URLs of articles from the positive, neutral and negative portions of this graph.
